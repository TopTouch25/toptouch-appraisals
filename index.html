Sharing Your Data

Once you have completed and saved your appraisal, export your data and send the file to your manager or admin for consolidation.

  1. Complete your self-assessment and click Save Self-Assessment.
  2. Click Export My Data below to download your data as a file.
  3. Send the file to your manager via WhatsApp, email, or shared Google Drive.

How Data Collection Works

Each person's data lives in their own browser. Collect everyone's files and import them here to build the master view.

  1. Each staff member saves their self-assessment and exports a JSON file.
  2. Each Team Leader saves their team scores and exports their file.
  3. You collect all files (WhatsApp / email / Google Drive) and import them below.
  4. The Dashboard then shows all combined scores in one place.
Tip: You can import multiple files at once — select them all in one go. Duplicate records are automatically merged.
